Offline SOS

Do the next five minutes.

You may be offline, but you are not without options. Stand up, change location, and interrupt the loop now.

Immediate rescue steps

  1. Put the device face down or move it across the room.
  2. Stand up and leave the current room.
  3. Take ten slow breaths and drink water.
  4. Text or call your support contact if you can.
  5. Do one clean action for ten minutes: walk, shower, pray, clean, or stretch.

Truth to hold

There is therefore now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus.

Pressure is real, but it is not your master. You can take the next faithful step.
